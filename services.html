Thermal Imaging

Advanced detection of panel issues and hotspots

Advanced thermal imaging to detect panel issues and hotspots that are invisible to the naked eye. Our thermal imaging service uses state-of-the-art equipment to identify problems before they cause significant damage or efficiency loss.

Thermal imaging picks up hot spots on your solar panels. Hot spots occur where voltage gets stuck and heat builds up. This occurs where a cell is damaged and voltage is not flowing as it should. This damage may occur in 1. old panels where components degrade over time, 2. through physical damage which may have occurred during transportation or installation, 3. physical damage from storm activity such as hail or tree debris, 4. shading form excessive build up of foreign material, (eg, lichen, bird, bat droppings) or 5. from poor manufacturing processes. This is why it is imperative to keep your solar panels cleaned regularly and inspected at least annually. Not only to keep your panels working efficiently, but to also ensure that your warranty is maintained. Early detection through thermal imaging can save you thousands in repair costs.

We provide detailed thermal imaging reports with visual evidence of any issues found, helping you make informed decisions about maintenance and repairs.
