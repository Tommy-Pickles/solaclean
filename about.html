About Sola Clean

We are a local business serving Tweed Valley, Northern Rivers, and Gold Coast, dedicated to providing top-quality solar panel, window, and exterior cleaning services. Our team is passionate about helping you maximize your energy efficiency and maintain the beauty and safety of your property.

With years of experience in the industry, we understand the unique challenges of maintaining solar panels, windows, and exterior surfaces in the coastal environment. Our professional team uses industry-best practices and equipment to ensure your property looks its best and your solar panels perform optimally.

We pride ourselves on reliability, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. Every job is completed to the highest standards, ensuring your investment is protected and your property maintains its value.
